Smart Tally

  • Duration 70Hrs
  • Module 2 Module
  • Exam 2 Online Exam
  • Certificate

Smart Tally -Includes information on maintaining Trading & Non-Trading accounts using Tally Software. Trading – it’s an organization which purchases and sales goods. Eg: Malls , Wholesalers etc. Non-Trading – it’s an organization which provides various services and are called as Service Organizations. Eg: Hospitals , Colleges , Coaching classes etc.
